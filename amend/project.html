What is the Amend Project?

The U.S. Constitution, drafted in 1787, was written to be amended. But of more than twelve thousand constitutional amendments proposed on the floor of Congress and many thousands more proposed by the public, only twenty-seven have ever been ratified. Amend aims to compile, classify, and analyze the text of every one of these proposals, to recover a lost tradition of constitutional tinkering and to rekindle Americans’ constitutional imagination.
