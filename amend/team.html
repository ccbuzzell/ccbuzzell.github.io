Project Director

Jill Lepore

is the David Woods Kemper ‘41 Professor of American History and Affiliate Professor of Law at Harvard University. She is the author of more than a dozen books, including These Truths: A History of the United States, published as a trade edition in 2018 and soon to be available as a college textbook. A staff writer for The New Yorker, she is also the host of the podcast, The Last Archive. She is an elected fellow of the American Academy of Arts and Sciences, the American Philosophical Society and the recipient of a number of honorary degrees and awards. She has been a finalist for the National Book Award, the National Magazine Award, and the Pulitzer Prize (twice).
